# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Quick answers to common questions about the E-Wallet system.

---

## For Customers

### Getting Started

**Q: What is an E-Wallet?**  
A: It's a digital wallet where you can store money to shop faster and easier. Think of it like a prepaid account for our store.

**Q: How do I access my wallet?**  
A: Log in to your account and go to "My Account" → "My Wallet" or visit `/customer/e-wallet`.

**Q: Is there a fee to use the wallet?**  
A: No, using the wallet is free. However, your payment provider (credit card, PayPal) may charge their own fees.

**Q: Do I have to use the wallet?**  
A: No, it's optional. You can still pay with credit card, PayPal, or other methods at checkout.

---

### Adding Money (Top-up)

**Q: How do I add money to my wallet?**  
A: Go to "My Wallet" → Click "Top Up Wallet" → Choose amount → Select payment method → Complete payment.

**Q: How long does a top-up take?**  
A: Usually instant! Once your payment is confirmed, money appears in your wallet immediately.

**Q: What's the minimum I can add?**  
A: Check the top-up page for current limits. Usually around $10.

**Q: What's the maximum I can add?**  
A: Check the top-up page for current limits. Usually around $10,000.

**Q: Which payment methods can I use?**  
A: You can use any enabled payment gateway like credit cards, PayPal, etc. The wallet payment method itself is excluded.

**Q: My top-up failed. What should I do?**  
A: Check your payment method, try again, or contact customer support with your payment confirmation.

**Q: I paid but money didn't appear. Help!**  
A: Wait a few minutes, then refresh the page. If still missing after 1 hour, contact support with your payment confirmation.

---

### Shopping with Wallet

**Q: How do I pay with my wallet?**  
A: At checkout, select "Wallet" or "Pay with E-Wallet" as your payment method.

**Q: Can I use wallet for any order?**  
A: Yes, as long as you have enough balance and you're logged in.

**Q: What if I don't have enough money?**  
A: You can either add more money to your wallet or choose a different payment method.

**Q: Can I use wallet + another payment method?**  
A: Currently no. You must pay the full amount with one method.

**Q: Is wallet payment secure?**  
A: Yes! Your wallet is protected by your account password and our security measures.

---

### Withdrawing Money

**Q: How do I withdraw money?**  
A: Go to "My Wallet" → Click "Withdraw" → Enter amount → Choose payment method → Provide details → Submit request.

**Q: How long does a withdrawal take?**  
A: 
- Review: 1-3 business days
- Bank Transfer: 3-5 business days after approval
- PayPal: 1-2 business days after approval

**Q: What's the minimum I can withdraw?**  
A: Check the withdrawal page for current limits. Usually around $10.

**Q: Can I cancel a withdrawal request?**  
A: Only before it's approved. Contact customer support immediately if you need to cancel.

**Q: My withdrawal was rejected. Why?**  
A: Common reasons: incorrect payment details, suspicious activity, or verification needed. Check your email for explanation.

**Q: Where does the money go?**  
A: To the bank account or PayPal email you provided. Make sure details are correct!

**Q: Are there withdrawal fees?**  
A: Check with customer support. Some payment methods may have fees.

---

### Refunds

**Q: How do refunds work?**  
A: When you return an item, the refund automatically goes to your wallet.

**Q: Can I get a refund to my credit card instead?**  
A: No, all refunds go to your wallet. You can then withdraw the money if needed.

**Q: How long until I see the refund?**  
A: Usually within 1-3 business days after the return is processed.

**Q: What if I don't want money in my wallet?**  
A: You can request a withdrawal to get the money back to your bank account.

---

### Transactions & Balance

**Q: How do I check my balance?**  
A: Go to "My Wallet" - your balance is displayed at the top.

**Q: Where can I see my transaction history?**  
A: Go to "My Wallet" and scroll down to "Transaction History".

**Q: What does each transaction type mean?**  
A:
- **Top Up**: You added money
- **Payment**: You paid for an order
- **Refund**: You received a refund
- **Withdrawal**: You withdrew money
- **Admin Adjustment**: Staff adjusted your balance

**Q: Can my balance go negative?**  
A: Usually no. You can only spend what you have.

**Q: I see a transaction I didn't make. What should I do?**  
A: Contact customer support immediately and change your password.

---

### Security

**Q: Is my money safe?**  
A: Yes! Your wallet is secured with encryption and protected by your account password.

**Q: What if someone hacks my account?**  
A: Enable two-factor authentication, use a strong password, and contact us immediately if you suspect unauthorized access.

**Q: Should I keep a lot of money in my wallet?**  
A: Only keep what you plan to spend soon. Don't store large amounts unnecessarily.

**Q: Can I share my wallet with family?**  
A: No, each wallet is personal to your account. Don't share login details.

## For Administrators

### General Management

**Q: How do I access the E-Wallet admin panel?**  
A: Admin Panel → E-Wallet menu in the sidebar.

**Q: What permissions do I need?**  
A: You need specific E-Wallet permissions assigned to your admin role. Check with your system administrator.

**Q: Can I see all customer wallets?**  
A: Yes, go to E-Wallet → Wallets to see all customer wallets and balances.

**Q: How do I search for a specific wallet?**  
A: Use the search box in the Wallets page. Search by customer name or email.

---

### Balance Adjustments

**Q: When should I adjust a customer's balance?**  
A: For customer service compensation, promotional credits, fixing errors, or special circumstances.

**Q: How do I adjust a balance?**  
A: E-Wallet → Wallets → Find customer → Click "Adjust Balance" → Choose Credit/Debit → Enter amount and reason → Confirm.

**Q: Do I need to notify the customer?**  
A: Yes! Always inform the customer why their balance was adjusted.

**Q: Can I undo a balance adjustment?**  
A: No, but you can make a reverse adjustment. Document both adjustments clearly.

**Q: What should I write in the reason field?**  
A: Be specific. Example: "Compensation for delayed order #12345" not just "Compensation".

---

### Top-ups

**Q: What if a customer's top-up is stuck in "Pending"?**  
A: Check if payment was received. If yes, manually complete it: E-Wallet → Top-ups → Find top-up → Click "Complete".

**Q: Should I cancel failed top-ups?**  
A: Failed top-ups are automatically marked as failed. Only cancel pending ones if requested.

**Q: Customer says they paid but wallet not credited. What do I do?**  
A: Check Top-ups list for their request. If found and payment confirmed, complete it manually.

---

### Withdrawals

**Q: How quickly should I process withdrawals?**  
A: Aim for 1-3 business days for review and approval.

**Q: What should I verify before approving?**  
A: Check: sufficient balance, correct payment details, no suspicious activity, customer identity (for large amounts).

**Q: When should I reject a withdrawal?**  
A: Incorrect details, suspicious activity, verification issues, or policy violations.

**Q: What happens when I reject a withdrawal?**  
A: Money is automatically refunded to the customer's wallet, and they receive a notification.

**Q: Do I need to manually send the money?**  
A: Yes! Approving just changes the status. You must actually process the bank transfer or PayPal payment.

---

### Settings

**Q: Who can change E-Wallet settings?**  
A: Only admins with "e-wallet.settings" permission.

**Q: What happens if I disable E-Wallet?**  
A: Customers can't access wallets, but existing balances are preserved. Re-enable to restore access.

**Q: Should I allow negative balances?**  
A: Usually no, unless you have a specific credit/installment program.

**Q: How do I set withdrawal limits?**  
A: E-Wallet → Settings → Withdrawal Settings → Set min/max amounts → Save.

**Q: What are webhooks for?**  
A: Advanced feature for integrating with external systems. Consult with developers before enabling.

---

### Troubleshooting

**Q: Customer can't see wallet payment at checkout. Why?**  
A: Check: E-Wallet is enabled, Wallet payment method is enabled, customer is logged in, customer has sufficient balance.

**Q: Settings won't save. What's wrong?**  
A: Check your permissions, clear browser cache, or try a different browser.

**Q: Transaction is missing from history. What happened?**  
A: Check if it failed or was cancelled. Review all transaction statuses, not just completed ones.

**Q: Balance doesn't match transaction history. Help!**  
A: Review all transactions, check for failed ones, and verify the math. Contact technical support if still incorrect.

---

### Reports

**Q: How do I generate reports?**  
A: E-Wallet → Dashboard → Select date range → Choose report type → Export.

**Q: What reports are available?**  
A: Transaction summaries, top wallets, top-up trends, withdrawal patterns, and revenue reports.

**Q: Can I export transaction data?**  
A: Yes, use the export function in the Transactions page.

**Q: How often should I review reports?**  
A: Daily for pending items, weekly for trends, monthly for comprehensive analysis.

## Technical Questions

**Q: What currency does the wallet use?**  
A: The wallet uses your store's default currency.

**Q: Can customers have multiple wallets?**  
A: Currently no, each customer has one wallet.

**Q: How is balance stored?**  
A: In cents (integer) for precision. $10.50 is stored as 1050 cents.

**Q: What happens if a payment gateway fails?**  
A: Top-up is marked as failed. Customer can try again or use a different payment method.

**Q: Are transactions reversible?**  
A: No, but admins can make adjusting transactions to correct errors.

**Q: What's an idempotency key?**  
A: A technical feature that prevents duplicate transactions. You don't need to worry about it.

## Common Scenarios

### Scenario 1: Customer Overpaid

**Q: Customer accidentally added too much money. What should I do?**  
A: Customer can request a withdrawal for the excess amount, or you can process a refund if they prefer.

### Scenario 2: Duplicate Charge

**Q: Customer was charged twice for the same order. How to fix?**  
A: Adjust their balance by crediting the duplicate amount with reason "Refund for duplicate charge on order #XXXX".

### Scenario 3: Promotional Credit

**Q: How do I give promotional credits to multiple customers?**  
A: Currently, adjust each customer's balance individually. Document the promotion code in the reason field.

### Scenario 4: Suspicious Activity

**Q: I see unusual withdrawal patterns. What should I do?**  
A: Put withdrawal requests on hold, contact the customer for verification, and report to your security team.

### Scenario 5: Customer Forgot Password

**Q: Customer can't access wallet because they forgot password. Help?**  
A: Direct them to the password reset process. Wallet access is tied to account login.
