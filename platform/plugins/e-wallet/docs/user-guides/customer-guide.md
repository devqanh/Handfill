# Customer User Guide

Welcome to your E-Wallet! This guide will help you understand how to use your digital wallet for shopping and managing your money.

## What is E-Wallet?

Your E-Wallet is like a digital piggy bank where you can:
- 💰 Store money safely
- 🛒 Pay for purchases instantly
- 💳 Add money using your credit/debit card
- 💸 Withdraw money when needed
- 📊 Track all your transactions

---

## Getting Started

### Accessing Your Wallet

1. **Log in** to your account
2. Click on **"My Account"** in the menu
3. Select **"My Wallet"** or visit: `/customer/e-wallet`

You'll see:
- Your current balance
- Recent transactions
- Buttons to add or withdraw money

---

## Adding Money to Your Wallet (Top-up)

### Why Add Money?

Adding money to your wallet lets you:
- Shop faster (no need to enter card details every time)
- Get ready for sales and special offers
- Keep your shopping budget organized

### How to Add Money

**Step 1: Start Top-up**
1. Go to **My Wallet**
2. Click the **"Top Up Wallet"** button

**Step 2: Choose Amount**
- Click a quick amount button (e.g., $10, $25, $50)
- OR type your own amount
- You'll see the minimum and maximum amounts allowed

**Step 3: Review**
- Check the amount you're adding
- See what your new balance will be

**Step 4: Pay**
1. Click **"Continue to Payment"**
2. Choose your payment method (Credit Card, PayPal, etc.)
3. Complete the payment
4. Wait for confirmation

**Step 5: Done!**
- Your wallet is credited immediately
- You'll see the new balance
- You can start shopping!

### Top-up Tips

✅ **DO:**
- Add money before big sales
- Use quick amount buttons for speed
- Check your balance before shopping

❌ **DON'T:**
- Add more than you need
- Share your payment details
- Ignore confirmation emails

## Shopping with Your Wallet

### Using Wallet at Checkout

**Step 1: Add Items to Cart**
- Shop normally and add items to your cart

**Step 2: Go to Checkout**
- Click "Checkout" when ready

**Step 3: Select Wallet Payment**
1. Look for **"Wallet"** or **"Pay with E-Wallet"** option
2. Click to select it
3. You'll see:
   - Your current balance
   - Order total
   - Remaining balance after purchase

**Step 4: Complete Order**
- Click **"Place Order"**
- Money is deducted instantly
- You'll see order confirmation

### What If I Don't Have Enough Money?

If your wallet balance is less than the order total:
- You'll see a message: "Insufficient wallet balance"
- **Options:**
  1. Add more money to your wallet
  2. Choose a different payment method
  3. Remove some items from your cart

## Withdrawing Money

### When to Withdraw

You might want to withdraw money if:
- You have unused balance
- You received a refund
- You want to move money to your bank

### How to Withdraw Money

**Step 1: Request Withdrawal**
1. Go to **My Wallet**
2. Click **"Withdraw"** button

**Step 2: Enter Amount**
- Type the amount you want to withdraw
- Must be within the allowed limits
- Can't exceed your current balance

**Step 3: Choose Payment Method**
Select how you want to receive the money:
- **Bank Transfer**: Enter your bank details
- **PayPal**: Enter your PayPal email
- **Other**: Follow the instructions

**Step 4: Provide Details**
- For Bank Transfer:
  - Bank name
  - Account number
  - Account holder name
  - Routing number (if needed)
- For PayPal:
  - Your PayPal email address

**Step 5: Submit Request**
- Review all details carefully
- Click **"Submit Withdrawal Request"**
- You'll see a confirmation message

**Step 6: Wait for Approval**
- An administrator will review your request
- This usually takes 1-3 business days
- You'll receive an email when approved

**Step 7: Receive Money**
- Once approved, money is sent to your account
- Bank transfers may take 3-5 business days
- PayPal is usually faster (1-2 days)

### Withdrawal Status

Check your withdrawal status:
1. Go to **My Wallet**
2. Click **"Withdrawals"** tab
3. See all your withdrawal requests and their status:
   - **Pending**: Waiting for review
   - **Processing**: Approved, payment being sent
   - **Completed**: Money sent to you
   - **Rejected**: Request denied (money returned to wallet)

### Withdrawal Tips

✅ **DO:**
- Double-check your bank/PayPal details
- Keep withdrawal confirmation emails
- Wait for approval before expecting money

❌ **DON'T:**
- Request withdrawal of money you need urgently
- Provide incorrect payment details
- Submit multiple requests for the same amount

## Viewing Your Transaction History

### What You Can See

Your transaction history shows:
- Every time you added money
- Every purchase you made
- Every refund you received
- Every withdrawal you requested
- Admin adjustments (if any)

### How to View Transactions

1. Go to **My Wallet**
2. Scroll down to **"Transaction History"**
3. You'll see a list showing:
   - Date and time
   - Transaction type (Top-up, Payment, Refund, etc.)
   - Amount (+ for money in, - for money out)
   - Balance before and after
   - Description

### Understanding Transaction Types

| Type | What It Means | Effect on Balance |
|------|---------------|-------------------|
| 💰 Top Up | You added money | Increases ⬆️ |
| 🛒 Payment | You paid for an order | Decreases ⬇️ |
| 💵 Refund | Order was refunded | Increases ⬆️ |
| 💸 Withdrawal | You withdrew money | Decreases ⬇️ |
| ⚙️ Admin Adjustment | Staff adjusted your balance | Varies |

## Refunds

### How Refunds Work

When you return an item or cancel an order:
1. The refund is **automatically** added to your wallet
2. You'll see a transaction in your history
3. You can use the money for future purchases
4. OR request a withdrawal to get cash back

### Checking Refunds

1. Go to **My Wallet**
2. Look for transactions marked **"Refund"**
3. Click on it to see which order it's from

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is my money safe in the wallet?

Yes! Your wallet is:
- Secured with encryption
- Protected by your account password
- Monitored for suspicious activity
- Backed by the store's security measures

### How long does a top-up take?

- **Instant**: Most top-ups are instant
- You can shop immediately after payment is confirmed

### How long does a withdrawal take?

- **Review**: 1-3 business days
- **Bank Transfer**: 3-5 business days after approval
- **PayPal**: 1-2 business days after approval

### Can I cancel a withdrawal request?

- **Before approval**: Contact customer support
- **After approval**: Cannot be cancelled

### What happens if I enter wrong bank details?

- Money may be returned to your wallet
- Or sent to wrong account (hard to recover)
- **Always double-check your details!**

### Can I have a negative balance?

- Usually **NO** - you can't spend more than you have
- Some stores allow it for special programs
- Check with customer support

### Are there any fees?

- **Top-up**: Usually no fees (check payment method)
- **Payments**: No fees
- **Withdrawals**: Check with customer support
- **Refunds**: No fees

### What if I see a transaction I didn't make?

1. **Don't panic**
2. Check if it's a legitimate purchase you forgot
3. Contact customer support immediately
4. Change your password

### Can I transfer money to another customer?

- This depends on store settings
- Check with customer support
- Some stores allow peer-to-peer transfers

## Tips for Using Your Wallet

### 💡 Smart Shopping Tips

1. **Add money before sales** - Be ready to grab deals
2. **Keep some balance** - For quick purchases
3. **Check balance regularly** - Know what you can spend
4. **Use for frequent purchases** - Faster checkout

### 🔒 Security Tips

1. **Use a strong password** - Mix letters, numbers, symbols
2. **Don't share login details** - Keep your account private
3. **Log out on shared devices** - Protect your wallet
4. **Check transactions regularly** - Spot any issues early
5. **Keep confirmation emails** - For your records

### 💰 Money Management Tips

1. **Set a budget** - Only add what you plan to spend
2. **Don't overfund** - Keep most money in your bank
3. **Withdraw unused balance** - If you're not shopping
4. **Track your spending** - Review transaction history
